# Digital Literacy Project – CSE0001 | VIT Bhopal

A structured portfolio of digital literacy tasks covering professional online 
presence, cybersecurity awareness, email communication, and coding platform proficiency.

## Tasks

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
An infographic summarizing core digital literacy concepts, designed using Canva.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and HackerRank developed as part of this project.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Beginner-level problem-solving on HackerRank and a Google Form survey on digital literacy awareness.
google form link [https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdf4S5lDr0wQWEbHbgmMskfrHWuJEkoIpFw3P-JxVQJ-djP6g/viewform?usp=dialog]

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette
Professional email drafts and a social media etiquette checklist.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
A case study on a real-world cybercrime incident and a prevention checklist.
