SOCIAL MEDIA ETIQUETTE

DO’s:
- Use respectful and professional language
- Think before posting or commenting
- Protect your personal information
- Verify information before sharing
- Be mindful of your digital footprint

DON’Ts:
- Do not share fake or unverified information
- Avoid using offensive or abusive language
- Do not overshare personal details
- Avoid engaging in online arguments
- Do not post inappropriate content
