CYBERCRIME PREVENTION CHECKLIST

- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links
- Do not share personal or banking information online
- Always verify website URLs before entering sensitive data
- Keep your software, apps, and devices updated
- Install and use antivirus or security tools
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi networks
- Regularly monitor your bank and online accounts for unusual activity
- Do not respond to suspicious emails or messages asking for urgent action

For reporting cybercrime in India:
Website: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
